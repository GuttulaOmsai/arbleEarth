Bamboo has a rich cultural heritage in various parts of the world, especially in Asian countries like China, Japan, India, and Southeast Asian nations. It has been used for centuries in traditional practices, art, construction, and as a material for various handicrafts. In India bamboo is used in day-to-day activities. It is recorded that bamboo is a part of life of ancient Indian cultures dating back to 3,000 years BC.

WHY BAMBOO

Expand our product range to offer a comprehensive selection of bamboo products, including household items, fashion accessories, furniture, construction materials, and more.

Bamboo is widely used in rural and eco-friendly construction practices in India. It is used for building houses, bridges, and even in modern architectural designs due to its strength, flexibility, and sustainability.

Despite its immense potential, the bamboo sector in India has faced challenges such as lack of infrastructure, inadequate market linkages, and limited awareness about the benefits of bamboo cultivation and utilisation.

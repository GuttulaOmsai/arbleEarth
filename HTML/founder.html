MEET THE FOUNDER

TSB Venkat Reddy

Mr. TSB Venkat Reddy is from agriculture background family and very passionate about farming. He did his graduation (B. Com) from Guntakal. He is a qualified professional management person and completed his ICWA in 1987.

He has experience in finance and management gained by 40 years of experience in various capacities. His core experience is in project development, dealing with banks for funding for various infra structure projects, accounts and taxation. Mr. TSB Venkat Reddy extended his services to a big industrial group as Vice President for more than 34 years. He is one of the directors in one pharma company and a software company.


As Director of AEPL he is leading the company to prosperous designation. He is wholly responsible to coordinate, manage, supervise various sections of AEPL with his management skills.

Mr. Raghu Ram is an agronomist by academics and agriculture consultant by profession. He is a B.Sc agriculture graduate and did post graduate diplomas in medicinal & aromatic plants, agriculture and irrigation management from various institutions.

He is a expert in Vedic Farming practices. He established SAMPADA FARMS & CONSULTANTS at Secunderabad in 1991 and extended consultancy services to many agri projects.

He extended his consultancy services in 9 other countries for multiple agri projects. He is having dairy farm management skills also and helped many farmers in establishing dairy farms.
